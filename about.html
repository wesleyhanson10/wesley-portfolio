Biomedical Engineering Innovator

My academic journey has been shaped by hands-on experience in hardware design, signal processing, and product development—combining theoretical knowledge with practical problem-solving.

Core Expertise

  • Hardware & Design: SolidWorks CAD modeling, circuit design, PCB layout, Design for Manufacturability (DFM), and geometric dimensioning
  • Software & Analysis: Python, MATLAB, Arduino, signal processing, data analysis, and machine learning
  • Product Development: Prototyping, validation, requirements traceability, and project management (Jira, CAPM)

Why Biomedical Engineering?

I'm fascinated by the intersection of engineering and healthcare. The opportunity to design devices that improve lives, streamline diagnostics, or enhance patient outcomes drives my work. I believe that thoughtful engineering combined with empathy for users can create meaningful innovations in the medical field.
